Indomie

Description

Read more to learn how to make the popular Malaysian instant noodles, Indomie!
It's really easy, you could even teach a monkey how to make it!

Ingredients

  • A pack of indomie
  • An egg
  • Cooking Oil
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. In a sauce pan, bring water to boil and add the indomie noodles
  2. Let it be for 3 minutes, then drain water and mix noodles with sauce packets
  3. On a frying pan, cook an egg to put over your indomie noodles and serve with sliced green onions on top!
